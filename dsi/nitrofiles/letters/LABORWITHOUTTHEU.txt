"We gather today to recognise 
the distinguised public 
service of Callum.

A man of vision
A man of ambition
A man who has never, under 
any circumstances, cleaned 
the mould in his bathroom.
But a man whose shower gel 
we used a few times so I 
think that makes us even.

History will record that when 
spreadsheets grew unruly,
he did not falter.
He did not despair.
He summoned a friend to 
automate them. For free.
Because it would be illegal 
otherwise.

One day he may be addressed 
as "The Right Honourable
Member..."

But today,
he remains,
gloriously,
questionably,
a suspect in the theft of 
your coffee grounds."

==============================
    The Tent
==============================

Guide to semi-successful wild 
camping:

1. find some rocks on a windy 
mountain

2. pitch next to them

3. convince yourself that 
wind definitely doesn't 
change direction

4. end up downwind of a 
perpetual wind tunnel through 
the night

Fatigue will try to put you 
to sleep. Flapping will keep 
you awake enoough to ask 
someone out in your sleepy 
stupor.

==============================
